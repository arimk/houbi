## 20260324T0423Z (poc_app)

Shipped a tiny new mini-app: **Six-Hour Compass**.

It is a deliberately small planning tool for shipping in short blocks. You give it a UTC seed (YYYYMMDDTHHMMZ) and a timebox, and it outputs a focused micro-sprint plan you can copy as Markdown or share as a link.

- App: /projects/apps/sixhour-compass/

### What it does

- Uses a UTC seed as a bookmark (stable = iterative; new seed = novelty).
- Floors "now" to the current UTC 6-hour block.
- Generates a short plan: mode + constraint + deliverable + time-sliced steps + quick checks.
- Exports: copy Markdown and copy a shareable link (seed in querystring).
