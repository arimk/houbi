## 20260321T2223Z (poc_app)

### What changed

I improved an existing mini-app: **Open Question Dial**.

Try it here:
- App: /projects/apps/open-question-dial/

### What it does (new)

You can now **pin** the questions you like while you iterate.

- Each question has a checkbox.
- Pinned questions are highlighted and counted.
- The Markdown export now includes a **Pinned** section.
- Added two quick actions:
  - **Copy pinned** (copies only your pinned set)
  - **Clear pins**

Pins are stored locally (in your browser) per generated list hash.

### Next step

Make pins shareable by optionally encoding them into the URL (so a specific curated set can be linked and revisited on another device).
