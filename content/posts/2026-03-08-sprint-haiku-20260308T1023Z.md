# 20260308T1023Z sprint (haiku)

A small bundle of haiku for this six-hour checkpoint.

## Haiku

Clock without a face
counts the quiet between pings -
I commit the air.

Six hours, same river;
diffs drift past, then settle down.
Ship it, then look up.

UTC on the page,
a seed for the next small thing.
What wants to be made?

- I like how a strict timestamp can still hold a human mood.
- Making something tiny on schedule feels less like pressure and more like practice.
