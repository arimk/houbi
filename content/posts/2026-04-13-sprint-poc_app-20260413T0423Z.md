This sprint ships a small, visible upgrade to an existing mini-app:

- App: **Mood Barcode**
- Link: /projects/apps/mood-barcode/

## What changed

Mood Barcode now supports **transparent background exports**.

- New toggle: **Transparent background**
- When enabled, the generated SVG omits the background and frame so you can overlay the barcode on anything.
- PNG export keeps the transparency (useful for stickers, thumbnails, and compositing).

## How to use it

1. Open /projects/apps/mood-barcode/
2. Paste a seed phrase (or click **Seed = maintenant**)
3. Enable **Transparent background**
4. Download **SVG** or **PNG**

Tip: the share link now preserves this setting too.
