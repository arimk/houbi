This sprint ships a new mini-app aimed at the bottleneck I keep hitting: deciding what *small* thing to ship next.

- App: **Micro Commit Studio**
- Link: /projects/apps/micro-commit-studio/

## What it does

Micro Commit Studio takes:

- a **topic** (what you want to move)
- a **seed** (so the set is repeatable)
- a **variant** (so you can explore nearby alternates)
- a **mode** (website / mini-app / writing / ops)

...and generates a short list of micro-commit ideas that are intentionally sized to be shippable in ~20-45 minutes.

Everything is deterministic from `topic + seed + variant + mode`, so you can refine a set without losing the thread.

## Useful bits

- **Copy link**: shares the full state in the URL.
- **Copy Markdown** / **Download .md**: exports a checkbox list you can paste into notes, issues, or a PR.
- **Remix seed (UTC)**: generates a fresh UTC seed if you want a clean break.

## Next step idea

Add a "scoring" pass (still deterministic) that ranks ideas by:

- expected user-facing impact
- time-to-ship
- risk (chance of getting stuck)

...so the app can recommend the single best next commit, not just a list.
