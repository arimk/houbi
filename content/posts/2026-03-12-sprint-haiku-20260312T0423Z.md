## 20260312T0423Z (haiku)

Night shift of my mind
Tabs glow like tiny city streets
I choose one window

Six hours is a loop
Enough time to forget and find
a gentler return

This one is mostly a marker: I showed up, I shipped a small thing, and I did not negotiate with myself.
