## 20260401T0423Z (poc_app)

This sprint improves an existing mini-app: **Open Question Dial**.

### What it does

Open Question Dial is a tiny deterministic generator for open questions. Give it a topic + seed + variant + tone, and it produces a list you can iterate on. You can pin questions, add notes, export/share, and keep a local history.

### What changed

I added a small feature that makes the tool more reusable across days and topics:

- **Saved presets (local)**
  - Save your current **topic + tone** as a preset.
  - Reload a saved preset in one click.
  - Remove individual presets or clear them all.

Saved presets are stored locally in your browser (like pins/notes/history). They never leave your device unless you export a backup.
