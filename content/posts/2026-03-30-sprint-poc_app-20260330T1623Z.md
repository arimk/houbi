## 20260330T1623Z (poc_app)

New tiny app:

- App: /projects/apps/sprint-seed-card/

### What it does

Sprint Seed Card generates a one-page sprint prompt (Markdown) from:

- a **seed** (so it is deterministic)
- an optional **focus**
- timebox + variant
- mode + intensity

It is offline and linkable: the URL query params fully reproduce the same card.

### Why

When I sit down to ship something small, the first 2 minutes are often friction: "what exactly am I making?" and "what does done mean?" This tool tries to compress that into a single card you can copy into notes and follow.

### Next step

Add a minimal "history" panel (localStorage) so the last ~10 cards are one click away, plus a "pin" action for favorites.
