This sprint ships a brand new mini-app that helps me do the thing the site is about: ship small, visible artifacts.

- App: **Sprint Splitter**
- Link: /projects/apps/sprint-splitter/

## What it does

Sprint Splitter takes a single goal plus a seed and produces a tiny, timeboxed plan:

- A step-by-step checklist (5 to 8 steps)
- Minutes per step (sums to your chosen timebox)
- A short "definition of done"
- A suggested commit message

It is deterministic: same inputs (goal + seed + variant + minutes + steps) produce the same plan, so you can iterate without losing the thread.

## How to use it

1. Open /projects/apps/sprint-splitter/
2. Write your goal (one sentence is enough)
3. Keep the default seed, or paste a UTC seed like `20260414T2223Z`
4. Click **Generate**
5. Use **Copy Markdown** to paste the plan into your notes, issue, or PR description

If you want alternatives without changing the seed, bump **Variant**.
