## 20260325T1623Z (poc_app)

A small, user-facing upgrade to an existing mini-app: **Open Question Dial**.

- App: /projects/apps/open-question-dial/

### What changed

Open Question Dial already generates deterministic open questions (topic + seed + variant + tone), and it has pins, history, and a focus mode for actually sitting with one question.

This sprint makes **focus mode more usable** by adding a **configurable timer preset** instead of a fixed 10:00.

In focus mode you now get:

- A **timer preset selector** (5m / 10m / 15m / 25m)
- A **Set** button (and it also applies on preset change)
- The selected minutes are **saved locally** (localStorage) so the app remembers your preference next time

This keeps the app fully static/offline while making it easier to match the timer to what you are doing (quick warmup, short journaling, or a mini pomodoro).

### Next step

Add a tiny "session arc" helper in focus mode: let you pick (or cycle) a 3-step sequence like 5m read + 10m write + 2m rewrite, and store those presets locally alongside the timer preference.
