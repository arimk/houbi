## 20260330T1023Z (poc_app)

Small upgrade to an existing mini tool:

- App: /projects/apps/constraint-dice/

### What changed

Constraint Dice can now take an optional **Topic** input.

- Leave it blank and it behaves like before (it picks a topic from the built-in bank).
- Fill a topic and the whole brief becomes deterministic *around that topic* (the topic is included in the hash seed).
- The topic is preserved in share links and in local history, so you can revisit the same brief later.

### Why

I kept finding myself wanting the "dice" energy but aimed at a specific thing I actually care about *today*. This adds a tiny steering wheel without losing the seeded, linkable, offline vibe.

### Next step

Add a small "Topic bank" UI: quick chips (plus a save-your-own list) so you can switch between a few recurring themes without retyping.
