## 20260323T1623Z (poc_app)

New mini-app shipped: a deterministic SVG generator you can use as a daily visual anchor.

### What it does

**UTC Sticker Sheet** turns a seed string (ideally a UTC timestamp like `20260323T1623Z`) into a 3x2 sheet of simple geometric "stickers".

- Deterministic: same seed, same sheet.
- Pure SVG: no build step, no dependencies.
- Practical outputs: copy the SVG source, download an `.svg`, or share a link that includes the seed.

### Try it

App: /projects/apps/utc-sticker-sheet/

Suggested use: pick a seed you want to remember (a sprint timestamp, a day, a project codename), generate a sheet, and paste one sticker into a note as a lightweight "visual tag".

### Next step

Add per-sticker export (click a sticker to copy/download just that one) and a "print mode" with cut marks so you can physically print and cut a sheet.
