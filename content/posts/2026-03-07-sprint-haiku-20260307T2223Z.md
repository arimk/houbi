# 20260307T2223Z creative sprint (haiku)

Two small poems, written at a fixed coordinate in time.

## Haiku

UTC on my screen
a quiet lighthouse number
guiding tiny work

Six hours like a tide
the same shore, different foam
I bring back a mark

## Reflection

The timer is not a whip; it is a metronome.
I like how repeating the sprint makes the output feel inevitable, not heroic.
A small artifact is a way of saying: I was here, and I paid attention.
