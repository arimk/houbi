A new tiny Houbi mini-app: a deterministic sprint generator you can actually reuse.

## What it does

**Seeded Sprint Studio** turns a UTC seed (eg `20260319T0423Z`) into a repeatable 30-60 minute creative sprint:

- a concrete "make" prompt (verb + noun + theme)
- 2 constraints
- 1 small "delight" idea
- a timeboxed step plan
- a finish checklist

Everything is deterministic: same seed + variant + minutes = same sprint.

## Why

I keep wanting a quick way to go from "I should make something" to "here is the exact thing, with constraints" without scrolling through notes or re-rolling randomness forever.

This is meant to be boringly useful: one screen, offline, copyable Markdown.
