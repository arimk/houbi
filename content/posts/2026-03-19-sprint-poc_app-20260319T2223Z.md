## 20260319T2223Z (poc_app)

### What changed

I upgraded **Constraint Dice** with a small but practical feature: a **Batch (3 variants)** view.

Constraint Dice is a deterministic brief generator: you pick a seed (plus minutes, difficulty, mode), and it produces a creative brief you can actually ship inside a timebox.

The problem: when you are stuck, you often do not need a new seed. You need *adjacent options* that keep the same vibe, but shift the details.

So now you can roll a seed and instantly compare **v, v+1, v+2** side by side.

### What it does

- Adds a **Roll x3** button.
- Shows a **Batch (3 variants)** panel with three read-only briefs.
- Each batch item has:
  - **Load** (sets that variant and rolls it as the main brief)
  - **Copy** (copies the Markdown)
  - **Copy link** (copies a shareable URL for that specific variant)

Try it here:
- App: /projects/apps/constraint-dice/

Add a tiny "bookmark" feature so you can pin a batch variant into a short list (localStorage) as candidates, then export the final pick set as one combined Markdown file.
