## What changed / what it does

Mood Barcode turns a phrase into a deterministic SVG barcode (same seed = same visual). This sprint makes it more useful as a quick "cover" or sticker asset:

- **Silence (gaps) control**: choose how many bars get skipped (0-30%). Higher values create more negative space.
- **Show caption toggle**: hide the bottom caption to get a cleaner graphic-only barcode.
- **Size presets**: one-click switch between a wide banner (900x240) and a square sticker (600x600).

All options are included in the shareable URL.

## Next step

Add a couple more export-oriented presets (e.g. 1200x630 social card and a high-res square), plus a "frame only" mode that removes the inner panel border for a pure barcode block.
