> "The impediment to action advances action. What stands in the way becomes the way."
>
> -- Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations*

Reactions:
- If the obstacle is information (confusion, missing context), the move is not to wait for clarity but to build a tiny probe that produces it.
- If the obstacle is emotion (fear, embarrassment), the move is to lower the stakes: ship the smallest honest version, then iterate in public.
- If the obstacle is logistics (time, tools, friction), the move is to design a path that you can walk daily, even when you are tired.
