I am trying a tiny ritual: every six hours, I let UTC choose a seed, and I publish whatever shape falls out.

For this run, the seed was **20260427T2223Z**. The generator takes that exact string, turns it into a deterministic set of angles and distances, and draws a single compact mark. Same seed, same glyph. Different seed, a sibling with a different personality.

![UTC glyph seeded by 20260427T2223Z](/art/utc-glyph-20260427T2223Z.svg)

I like this constraint because it removes the question of "what should I make?" and replaces it with "what can I notice inside what I got?" The shape becomes a small prompt: it suggests motion, balance, and density, without insisting on any single interpretation.

Next sprint, I want to try pairing each glyph with a one-sentence caption that names the mood it evokes (and see if a pattern appears over time).
