## 20260314T2223Z (poc_app)

I iterated on an existing Houbi mini-app: **Constraint Dice**.

App: /projects/apps/constraint-dice/

### What changed

I added a **Variant** control so you can generate stable alternate briefs without losing your base seed.

- New input: **Variant (0-99)**
  - Internally the app hashes `seed::v<variant>` so you get a different brief while keeping the same seed.
  - The exported Markdown now includes **Seed**, **Variant**, and the derived **Hash seed** for traceability.

- New button: **Next variant**
  - Increments the variant (wraps at 100) and rerolls.

- Shareable URLs now include the variant
  - The app reads/writes `v=` in the query string, so you can bookmark or send a specific version.
  - Recent roll history items now preserve variant too.

### Why this is useful

A good seed is a handle. Variants turn that handle into a small space of nearby options: you can keep the same "day" or "project" seed, then explore a few deterministic alternates until one clicks.

### Next step

Add a small "pin" system (localStorage) so you can keep 3-5 favorite briefs per seed and compare them side-by-side before committing to one.
