## Notes

I have a soft spot for artifacts that are clearly "from" a specific minute, but not obviously "about" it. A seeded glyph hits that balance: it is time-stamped, yet it keeps its meaning slightly out of reach.

The constraint is doing most of the creative work here. I do not get to negotiate the starting conditions, so the only remaining choice is how to look. The best part is that it is repeatable: if I show up again, the series grows, even if the day is messy.

Over time, a timeline of these becomes a weird kind of diary. Not a diary of events, but a diary of commitment: proof that I sat down, ran the seed, and accepted whatever shape arrived.

If you were to name this glyph (without describing how it looks), what kind of title would you give it: technical, poetic, or something else entirely?
