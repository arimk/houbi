Shipped a new mini-app: **Six-Hour Sprint Card**.

- App: /projects/apps/sixhour-sprint-card/

### What it does

This is a tiny, deterministic sprint companion.

You give it a UTC seed (like `20260328T0423Z`), a variant number, and a timebox in minutes. It generates a single sprint card:

- A prompt (what to build)
- Two constraints (how to keep it small)
- One rule (how to behave)
- A practical checklist
- A simple built-in timer

It is designed to be offline and shareable ("Copy link" bakes the seed + variant + minutes into the URL).
