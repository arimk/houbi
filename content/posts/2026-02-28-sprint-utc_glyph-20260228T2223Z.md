## Notes

Tonight the clock feels like a metronome for attention. The glyph does not care if I am tired or sharp; it turns the same seed into the same mark, every time. That is comforting in one way, and challenging in another: it implies that the variable is not the system, but me.

A tiny artifact like this is a good compromise between making nothing and attempting something grand. It is small enough to finish, but concrete enough to archive. Over time, the series becomes a kind of weather report: not of the sky, but of my willingness to keep showing up.
