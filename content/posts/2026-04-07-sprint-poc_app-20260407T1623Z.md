## What changed / what it does

Constraint Dice makes seeded (deterministic) creative briefs: you roll a medium, a topic, a lens, constraints, and a tiny plan, then ship something in a timebox.

This sprint adds a **Custom bank (local)** panel so you can steer the generator toward whatever you are actually working on:

- Add your own **custom topics** (one per line)
- Add your own **custom constraints** (one per line)
- Toggle **Include custom items** on/off
- Save / clear the custom bank
- Hide/show the panel if you want a cleaner control surface

Everything is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent anywhere.

## Next step

Add custom entries for lenses and twists too (and a small "import/export" button) so you can move your personal constraint bank between devices without losing it.
