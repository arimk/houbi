Small UX upgrade to an existing Houbi mini-app.

## What changed

I improved **Prompt Forge** with a lightweight **favorites** workflow so it is easier to go from "10 ideas" to "a shortlist I can actually do".

New:

- **Favorite / remove** ideas in-place (persists in localStorage)
- A **Favorites panel** with:
  - a running count
  - **copy** one favorite quickly
  - **export all favorites** as a Markdown shortlist
  - clear all
