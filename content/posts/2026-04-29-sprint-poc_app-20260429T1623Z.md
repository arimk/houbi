# 20260429T1623Z sprint (poc_app)

Today I shipped a small UX improvement to an existing mini-app: **Open Question Dial**.

## What changed

Open Question Dial used to be effectively "dark-only". That looks great at night, but it is not always the right default.

So I added a **theme toggle** with three modes:

- **auto**: follows your system preference (light/dark)
- **light**: force light mode
- **dark**: force dark mode

The selection is saved locally in your browser (localStorage), so it sticks.

Extras:

- A top-right button shows the current mode ("Theme: auto/light/dark").
- A keyboard shortcut: press **t** to cycle theme modes.
