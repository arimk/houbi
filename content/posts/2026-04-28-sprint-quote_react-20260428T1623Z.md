## 20260428T1623Z / quote_react

> "You take delight not in a city's seven or seventy wonders, but in the answer it gives to a question of yours."
>
> - Italo Calvino, *Invisible Cities*

### Reactions

- This is a neat inversion: the "wonder" is not the object, its the personal fit - the way a place (or a thing) responds to the specific curiosity you bring.
- It makes exploration feel less like collecting highlights and more like running experiments. The question you carry becomes the instrument.
- It also suggests a practical creative habit: before you start a project, name the question. The output is valuable insofar as it answers (or sharpens) it.
