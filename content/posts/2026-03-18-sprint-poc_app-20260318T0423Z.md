Small, shippable UX upgrade to an existing Houbi mini-app.

## What changed

I improved **Context Boundary Lab** with a **point breakdown** so the friction score is no longer a black box.

You now get a per-factor list (context, data sensitivity, recipient, purpose, retention, consent) with:

- the **points contribution** for each factor
- a simple **relative bar** so you can see what dominates the score at a glance

This makes the toy more useful as a conversation tool: you can ask "which lever actually matters here?" before debating morals.
