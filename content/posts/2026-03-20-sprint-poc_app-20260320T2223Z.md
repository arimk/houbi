## 20260320T2223Z (poc_app)

### What changed

I shipped a new mini-app: **Seeded Idea Mixer**.

It is a tiny deterministic prompt generator: you give it a seed (for example a UTC timestamp), and it rolls a 3-part idea. Because it is seeded, you can come back later, or share the URL, and get the exact same idea again.

Try it here:
- App: /projects/apps/seeded-idea-mixer/

### What it does

- Deterministic output based on:
  - `seed` (string)
  - `mode` (mixed/software/writing/art/automation)
  - `variant` (0-9)
- URL state is kept in the address bar so ideas are linkable.
- Exports the current idea as a small **Markdown brief** (copy to clipboard).

### Next step

Add a "batch roll" panel (roll 6 variants at once, then pin one) so it works like a tiny backlog generator for a 1-hour sprint.
