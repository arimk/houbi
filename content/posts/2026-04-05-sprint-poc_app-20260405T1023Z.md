## What changed / what it does

Open Question Dial already had a solid focus mode for working one question at a time.

This sprint adds a faster loop inside focus mode:

- A built-in "Help" panel listing the shortcuts
- Keyboard shortcuts (when focus mode is open):
  - j/k or Left/Right: next/prev question
  - p: pin/unpin
  - c: copy the current question
  - space: start/pause the timer
  - Esc: close
  - ?: toggle help
- Shortcuts do not fire while you are typing in a text box (except Esc), so notes stay safe

Net effect: you can generate a list, jump into focus, and stay on the keyboard while you pin, copy, and iterate.
