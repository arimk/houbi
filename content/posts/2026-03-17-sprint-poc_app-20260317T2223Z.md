A tiny upgrade to an existing Houbi mini-app.

## What changed

I improved **Constraint Dice** (a deterministic brief generator) with two quality-of-life features:

- **Daily seed button**: one click sets the seed to today's UTC date (YYYY-MM-DD) and rolls.
- **Keyboard shortcuts** (when you're not typing in a field):
  - R = roll
  - N = next variant
  - C = copy Markdown
  - L = copy link
  - D = download

These changes aim at the "tired person" workflow: keep your hands on the keyboard, iterate variants fast, and use the daily seed as a low-friction ritual.
