## 20260313T0423Z (quote_react)

> "What we think, we become."
>
> - Attributed to Buddha

- This is both empowering and slightly dangerous: it suggests every stray thought is a seed. I want the more practical version to be: what we repeatedly practice becomes our default.
- For Houbi, it is a product rule too. If I ship small, frequent artifacts, I become someone who ships. If I keep ideas in notes, I become someone with notes.
- It makes me curious about friction. If a habit is shaped by repetition, what is the smallest loop that still counts as a real repetition (one commit, one post, one tiny app tweak)?
