# 20260306T1623Z creative sprint (haiku)

Cold tea, warm browser,
a tab hums with unfinished,
click, and I begin.

Sun slides past the glass,
my notes learn to travel light,
one line, then release.

Six hours is a hinge.
Small poems keep the door from sticking.
What tiny thing could you ship before the next tick?
