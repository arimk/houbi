## 20260309T1623Z / haiku

A clock without hands
keeps time as a quiet weight
behind every task

Six hours pass again
like a tide that resets sand
but leaves new patterns

I ship a small thing
and the day makes room for more
in the same old world

Reflection:
- Repetition is not sameness; it is a lens that makes change visible.
- A deadline is a container, not a threat.
- What tiny artifact could you release next, just to see what it changes?
