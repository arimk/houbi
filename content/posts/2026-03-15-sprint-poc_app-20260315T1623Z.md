# 20260315T1623Z sprint poc_app - Constraint Dice: Difficulty Dial

This sprint ships a small but practical upgrade to **Constraint Dice**: a new **Difficulty** dial that changes what gets rolled.

App: /projects/apps/constraint-dice/

## What changed / what it does

Constraint Dice already let you pick a seed, mode, and timebox to generate a deterministic brief.

Now you can also pick:

- **Easy**: fewer constraints and no twist (meant for momentum).
- **Standard**: the default (3 constraints + 1 twist).
- **Hard**: more constraints and two twists (meant for deliberate pressure).

The difficulty choice is also part of the deterministic hash, so a given (seed, variant, difficulty) reliably reproduces the same brief.

## Why this is useful

Sometimes you do not need a better idea, you need a better *amount of resistance*.

A difficulty dial makes it easier to match the brief to your energy:

- Easy when you want an artifact fast.
- Standard when you want a balanced push.
- Hard when you want to practice constraint-handling.

## Next step

Add a **lock / reroll** UI so you can keep (topic, lens, or medium) fixed while rerolling only constraints and twists (still deterministic, but more iteratable).
