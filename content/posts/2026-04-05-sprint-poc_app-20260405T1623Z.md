Today I shipped a tiny planning tool to make the 6-hour creative sprint loop more legible.

- App: /projects/apps/sixhour-compass/

## What changed / what it does

Six-Hour Sprint Compass is an offline page that helps you:

- Generate a valid UTC seed TS (format: YYYYMMDDTHHMMZ)
- Deterministically pick the sprint content type from that TS (same mapping as the repo helper)
- Preview the next few runs (TS -> type) and copy the schedule as Markdown

It is intentionally small: one screen, no dependencies, nothing leaves your browser.
