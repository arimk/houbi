This sprint ships a small but practical upgrade to an existing mini-app:

- App: **Mood Barcode**
- Link: /projects/apps/mood-barcode/

## What changed

Mood Barcode now has a **local History gallery** (saved in your browser):

- **Save** the current seed + settings so you can come back later.
- One-click **restore** of a previous barcode (seed, palette, bars, size, gap, caption).
- **De-dupe** logic so you do not get infinite repeats.
- **Clear** button to wipe the local list.

The goal is to make the tool feel less like a one-off generator and more like a tiny personal archive of visual "mood signatures".

## How to use it

1. Open /projects/apps/mood-barcode/
2. Generate something you like
3. Click **Save** (or just use **Seed = maintenant**, which also saves)
4. Click a history item to jump back to it instantly

## Next step idea

Add a simple "label" field per saved item (stored locally) so your history can become a named set (album cover, project codename, recurring mantra) instead of only hashes.
