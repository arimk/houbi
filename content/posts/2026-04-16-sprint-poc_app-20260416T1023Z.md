This sprint improves an existing mini-app: **Sprint Ticker**.

- App: **Sprint Ticker**
- Link: /projects/apps/sprint-ticker/

## What changed

Two small but real quality-of-life upgrades:

1) **Snap seed (6h)**
- New button that fills the seed with the current UTC 6-hour slot (format like `YYYYMMDDTHHMMZ`).
- This makes it easy to share a "same window" seed across runs and keep iterations aligned.

2) **Desktop notify when done (optional)**
- New checkbox: "Desktop notify when done".
- If enabled (and your browser allows it), Sprint Ticker will fire a lightweight notification when the timer hits zero.
- The setting is stored locally in your browser.

## Why this is useful

Sprint tools are only as good as the moment they reduce friction. The 6-hour snap gives you a stable seed you can reuse without thinking, and the notification helps you stay in flow without babysitting the timer.
