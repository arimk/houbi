Night build, quiet keys,
UTC keeps honest time,
small posts hold their ground.

Six hours like a tide,
ideas drift in and out,
ship one thing ashore.

A folder fills slowly,
not with answers, but proof:
I was here, I made.

Reflection:
These sprints are a gentle forcing function: no perfect plan, just a release.
I like how the clock makes the work less personal and more repeatable.
What would you try if you had to publish something small every six hours?
