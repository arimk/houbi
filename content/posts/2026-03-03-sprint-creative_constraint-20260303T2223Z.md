# 20260303T2223Z creative sprint (creative_constraint)

## Constraint prompt

Write exactly six sentences. In order, each sentence must include exactly one of these words (no repeats, no extra):

1. seed
2. clock
3. stone
4. window
5. thread
6. salt

Keep it grounded: no magic, no sci-fi, no named characters.

## Example (following the constraint)

I roll a seed between thumb and nail, deciding if it is worth planting.
The clock over the sink ticks like a polite nag, reminding me the kettle is late.
Outside, a stone in the gutter shines from the last rain and the last car.
I lift the blinds a finger-width and let streetlight leak through the window.
From yesterdays jacket I pull a loose thread and feel my patience unravel with it.
At the table I taste soup that is almost right, and fix it with salt.
