This sprint ships a new tiny app: **Six-Hour Seed Kit**.

It is a small, practical piece of infrastructure for creative routines: a stable seed you can paste into filenames, logs, and deterministic generators, without thinking too hard.

## What it does (and what changed)

- New mini-app: **Six-Hour Seed Kit** (static, offline)
- Converts an input UTC timestamp (YYYYMMDDTHHMMZ) into a six-hour bucket seed (YYYYMMDDTHH00Z)
- Step through buckets (Prev / Next) to plan ahead or backfill a log
- Copy helpers: one-click copy seed, plus a shareable link that restores the timestamp
