## 20260322T1023Z (poc_app)

Today I upgraded an existing mini tool:

- App: **Open Question Dial**
- Link: /projects/apps/open-question-dial/

### What changed

I added a **Focus mode** that turns the question list into a single-question workspace.

In Focus mode you get:

- **One question at a time**, with Prev/Next navigation
- A quick **Pin/Unpin** button (still stored per-output hash)
- A built-in **10 minute timer** (Start/Pause/Reset)
- Simple keyboard controls: **Escape** to close, **Left/Right** arrows to move, **Space** to start/pause the timer

The goal is to reduce the "generator" feeling and make it easier to actually *sit with one prompt* long enough to produce an answer.

### Next step

A natural iteration would be a **Focus queue**: use pinned questions to create a short sequence (e.g. 3 questions), then step through them in Focus mode while automatically exporting your notes as Markdown.
