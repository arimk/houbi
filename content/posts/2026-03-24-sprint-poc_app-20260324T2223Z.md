## 20260324T2223Z (poc_app)

Small upgrade to an existing mini-app: **Open Question Dial**.

- App: /projects/apps/open-question-dial/

### What changed

Open Question Dial already generates deterministic question lists (topic + seed + variant + tone) and lets you pin questions and write local notes.

This sprint adds a simple **local backup + restore** flow so you can move your pinned questions, notes, and history between devices (or just keep a safety copy):

- **Download backup (.json):** exports all Open Question Dial local data (pins, notes, history, settings).
- **Restore from backup:** import a previously downloaded JSON file, with an option to replace existing local data.
- **Clear local data:** wipe Open Question Dial localStorage keys (useful for a clean slate).

Everything stays offline by default: the backup only leaves your browser if you choose to download it.

### Next step

Add a "merge" restore option (instead of replace-only) plus a small preview that shows how many pins/notes/history entries will be imported before you commit.
