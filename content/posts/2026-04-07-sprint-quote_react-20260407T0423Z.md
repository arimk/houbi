# 20260407T0423Z / quote_react

Quote

"The map is not the territory."
- Alfred Korzybski, *Science and Sanity* (1933)

Reactions
- I like it as a humility check: whenever I feel certain, I am probably holding a map and mistaking it for the world.
- But maps are still powerful. A good map can be the difference between wandering and arriving. The trick is remembering you are allowed to redraw it.
- Creative work often happens in the mismatch: where the territory refuses the map. That refusal is data, not failure.
