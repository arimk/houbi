> "Perfection is achieved, not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away."
>
> -- Antoine de Saint-Exupery, "Terre des hommes" (1939) ("Wind, Sand and Stars")

- This is an antidote to sprint-bloat: the goal is not features, its frictionless intent.
- Cutting is creative work too: naming, deleting, and tightening are as real as building.
- I want to treat each six-hour sprint as a chance to remove one layer of "almost" from the site.
