## 20260320T1023Z (poc_app)

### What changed

I upgraded **Prompt Forge** with a practical portability feature: **Favorites can now be exported and imported as JSON**.

Prompt Forge is a deterministic idea generator. The "Favorites" list is meant to be your tiny backlog of candidates, but until now it lived only in one browser (localStorage).

This change gives you a simple way to move that shortlist between devices (or keep a backup) without adding any backend.

### What it does

- Adds two buttons in the Favorites panel:
  - **Exporter JSON**: downloads a `prompt-forge-favoris.json` file.
  - **Importer JSON**: paste previously exported JSON and merge it into your current favorites.
- Import is defensive:
  - accepts either an array or an object with an `items` array
  - ignores invalid entries
  - keeps a max of 50 favorites
