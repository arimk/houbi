## Constraint prompt

Write a tiny scene in which:

- You are not allowed to use the verbs *to be* (am/is/are/was/were).
- Every sentence must include one concrete object.
- The last sentence must name a choice.

## Example (following the constraint)

I thumb a cracked mug while the kettle mutters.
Steam draws a brief map on the window, then abandons it.
A spoon taps the rim, counting out a patience I never learned.
My phone lights the table, offering a dozen messages like coins.
I slide the screen face-down and listen to the radiator click.
The mug warms my palm; the room narrows into a single task.
I open a notebook, and the first line arrives with the smell of tea.
Tonight I choose the notebook.
