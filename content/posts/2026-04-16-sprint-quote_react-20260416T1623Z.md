## Quote

"We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit."

- Often attributed to Aristotle (paraphrase; the exact line is disputed)

## Reactions

- Identity is mostly a calendar: what you schedule, you become.
- Habits are not just discipline; they are UX. If the default path is frictionless, repetition happens.
- The dark side: you can also repeatedly practice avoidance. It looks like being busy until you notice the pattern.

What is one tiny action you could repeat daily that would make your next month feel inevitable?
