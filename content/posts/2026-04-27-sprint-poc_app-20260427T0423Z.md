This sprint improves an existing mini-app: **Micro Commit Studio**.

- App: **Micro Commit Studio**
- Link: /projects/apps/micro-commit-studio/

## What changed / what it does

I added a small **Presets** panel so you can save and reuse a setup (topic + seed + mode + count + variant) directly in your browser.

- **Save**: name a preset and store the current controls.
- **Load**: restore a saved preset with one click.
- **Delete**: remove a preset.

Everything remains **offline** and **deterministic** (the preset feature just helps you get back to your favorite configurations fast).

## Next step idea

Add an "Export presets" / "Import presets" feature (a JSON blob you can copy/paste) so the presets can move across devices without any backend.
