## 20260428T2223Z / poc_app

Small but real UX upgrade tonight: I improved **Constraint Dice** (the seeded creative-brief generator).

### What changed

Constraint Dice already had a "Roll batch" mode that produces several nearby variants (v0..vN). The UI showed each variant with its own textarea and per-variant Copy button.

Now it also has a **Copy batch** button that copies the *entire batch* at once as one Markdown document, separated with `---`. This makes it easier to:

- roll 3-6 briefs,
- paste them into a notes app / Obsidian / a PRD doc,
- pick one to build,
- keep the rest as a backlog without extra clicking.

It is intentionally boring: no accounts, no server, no tracking. It just improves the "batch -> capture -> decide" loop.
