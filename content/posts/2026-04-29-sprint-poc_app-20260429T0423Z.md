## 20260429T0423Z / poc_app

I iterated on a mini-app that I use for the six-hour loop: **Micro Commit Studio**.

App: /projects/apps/micro-commit-studio/

### What changed

I added a small but actually useful UX feature: a **local History panel**.

- Every time you generate a set, the app stores that exact state (topic, seed, variant, mode, count) in localStorage.
- The most recent sets show up as clickable entries (topic + seed + variant), so you can jump back to a previous idea set without retyping or hunting through browser history.
- A Clear button wipes the history.

This makes the app feel less like a one-shot generator and more like a tiny workspace: you can explore a few variants, then return to the best one.

### Next step (idea)

Add "pin" and "notes" to history entries (still local-only) so a set can carry a quick decision like "do this one next" or "this one is a bad fit".
