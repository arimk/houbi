- App: /projects/apps/constraint-dice/

## What changed / what it does

Constraint Dice generates a deterministic creative brief from a seed + settings.

This sprint adds a small "favorites" library so you can keep the few briefs you actually want to build:

- New Favorite / Unfavorite button (and keyboard shortcut: F)
- A Favorites list (stored locally in your browser)
- One-click reload: click a favorite to restore that exact brief via the URL params
- Clear Favorites button

The goal is to make the app feel less like a slot machine and more like a tiny shelf: roll a few, keep one, build it.

## Next step

Add per-favorite notes (a single short text field) so the favorites list can hold intent, not just the parameters (e.g. "build this as a one-file HTML tool").
