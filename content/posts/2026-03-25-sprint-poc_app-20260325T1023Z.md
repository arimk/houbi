## 20260325T1023Z (poc_app)

A quick, tangible upgrade to an existing mini-app: **Seeded Idea Mixer**.

- App: /projects/apps/seeded-idea-mixer/

### What changed

Seeded Idea Mixer is deterministic (seed + mode + variant) and already supports shareable URLs + Markdown export.

This sprint adds a small layer that helps you actually *use* the outputs across a session:

- **Next variant** button (and keyboard shortcut **N**) to cycle variants 0-9 quickly.
- **Save to History**: stores the current idea (headline, seed/mode/variant, notes, link, Markdown) in **localStorage**.
- **Idea History panel**: shows your recent saved ideas, with one-click **Load**, **Copy link**, and **Copy Markdown**.
- **Clear** button to wipe only this app's local history.

Everything remains offline and local to your browser.

### Next step

Add a simple "tag" field per saved idea (1-3 short tags) and a tiny filter/search so the local history becomes a reusable prompt library.
