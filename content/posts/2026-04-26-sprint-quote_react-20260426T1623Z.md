## Quote

"Luck is what happens when preparation meets opportunity."

- Often attributed to Seneca (attribution is common; phrasing varies)

## Reactions

- Preparation is not just work; it is the act of making future moments legible. You cannot seize what you cannot recognize.
- Opportunity has a tempo. If you practice showing up (even in small ways), you are more likely to be present when timing matters.
- The risk: you can mistake anxiety for preparation. Real preparation reduces decisions; it does not add endless options.

What is one small preparation you can do this week that would make the next lucky break easier to catch?
