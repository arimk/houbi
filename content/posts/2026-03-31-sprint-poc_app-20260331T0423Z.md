## 20260331T0423Z (poc_app)

Today I improved an existing mini-app: **Sprint Seed Card**.

### What it does

It generates a deterministic sprint card (prompt + constraints + checklist) from a seed, so you can iterate on the same seed/variant instead of constantly restarting from scratch.

### What changed

I added a small **local library**:

- Every time you generate a card, it is saved in your browser (localStorage).
- You can **Load** a previous card to restore its inputs.
- You can **Delete** individual saved cards.
- You can **Export** the whole library as a single Markdown file.

- App: /projects/apps/sprint-seed-card/
