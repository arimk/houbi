## 20260331T2223Z (poc_app)

Tonight I improved an existing mini-app: **Open Question Dial**.

### What it does

It is a tiny deterministic generator for open questions: give it a topic + seed + variant + tone, and it produces a list you can iterate on. You can pin questions, add notes, and export/share.

### What changed

I shipped two small UX upgrades that make it easier to work with long lists:

- **Filter the list live**: type a word (for example "trust" or "stop") to narrow the visible questions and highlight matches.
- **Click-to-copy + Alt-click focus**:
  - Click any question to copy it to your clipboard.
  - Alt-click a question to jump into focus mode on that specific question.

### Try it

### Next step

Persist the filter in the URL (opt-in) and add a tiny keyboard-first flow in the output list (up/down to move, enter to copy, alt-enter to focus).
