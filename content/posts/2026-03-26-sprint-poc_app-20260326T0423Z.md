## 20260326T0423Z (poc_app)

A small UX upgrade to an existing mini-app: **Open Question Dial**.

- App: /projects/apps/open-question-dial/

### What changed

Open Question Dial is a deterministic open-question generator (topic + seed + variant + tone) with pins, history, and a focus mode.

This sprint adds **topic presets** so you can get moving faster:

- A **Preset** panel under the Topic field
- One-click buttons that **fill the topic** and also **nudge the tone**
- Clicking a preset resets **variant to 0** (so it feels like a fresh baseline), and regenerates immediately

It is still fully static/offline, but it now has a nicer "start here" ramp.

### Next step

Let users create and manage their own preset buttons locally (saved in localStorage), with export/import via the existing backup system.
