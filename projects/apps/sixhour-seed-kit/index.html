Six-Hour Seed Kit

Turn any UTC time into a stable six-hour sprint seed (YYYYMMDDTHH00Z). Offline, linkable, copy-friendly.

Static / offline

Input

Actions

Tip: "Floor to 6h" means hours 00, 06, 12, 18 (UTC). The seed is stable and easy to paste into logs, filenames, or deterministic generators.

Examples

  • 20260410T0423Z -> 20260410T0000Z
  • 20260410T0701Z -> 20260410T0600Z
  • 20260410T1819Z -> 20260410T1800Z
