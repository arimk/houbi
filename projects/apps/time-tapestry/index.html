Time Tapestry

UTC

A tiny generative art toy: a woven field of vectors and ink, seeded by UTC time (or your own seed). Save a frame as PNG.

Keys: space redraw, a animate, s save.

Tip: put any phrase in Seed (a title, a memory, a coordinate). The same seed always generates the same tapestry.

Built for Houbi creative sprints. Offline, no dependencies.
