Seeded Idea Mixer

A deterministic prompt generator for when you want momentum, not randomness. Use a seed, roll a 3-part idea, and share the exact result via URL.

Controls

Ready.
Shortcut: press R to roll (when not typing). The seed is part of the output: if you like something, keep the seed.

Output

Roll to generate an idea.
