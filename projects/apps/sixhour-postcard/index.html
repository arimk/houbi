Six-Hour Postcard

A tiny deterministic generator: give it a UTC seed + a short line, and it creates an SVG postcard you can copy, download, or link. Offline. No accounts. Just a small artifact.

Static / offline

Controls

Tip: keep the same seed to iterate visually; change only the line. Or keep the line and move the seed forward to make a daily series.
