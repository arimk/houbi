Open Question Dial

A tiny deterministic generator for open questions. Give it a topic + seed, and it produces a list you can iterate on. Offline, linkable, exportable.

Static / offline

Controls

Presets

Click a preset to fill the topic (and nudge the tone). You can still edit everything after.

Tip: Keep the seed if you want to refine the same list over time. Change variant when you want a nearby alternate. Remix makes a new seed from the current UTC time.

History

History is stored locally in your browser. It remembers topic + seed + settings so you can jump back to a previous list.

Backup

Backup includes pins, notes, and history. It never leaves your browser unless you download it.
